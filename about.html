About Advait Wisdom Awareness Foundation

We are a spiritual and educational nonprofit committed to sharing the timeless wisdom of Advaita Vedanta. Our foundation is grounded in the belief that true knowledge is experiential, universal, and transformative.

🌱 Our Mission

To awaken spiritual insight through the teachings of the Upanishads, Bhagavad Gita, and other Vedantic scriptures — making them accessible in modern formats.

🌟 Our Vision

A world guided by awareness, unity, and inner peace, where ancient wisdom meets scientific inquiry for holistic growth.

What We Do

  • 📖 Host webinars, lectures, and satsangs
  • 📚 Publish spiritual resources and video content
  • 🌐 Offer online courses and guided meditations
  • 🕉️ Conduct retreats and spiritual camps
